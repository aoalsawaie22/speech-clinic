Project Overview

A specialized web platform supporting children aged 2–5 who suffer from language delay caused by excessive screen time. The system transforms passive screen usage into active learning through guided interactive speech exercises and real-time professional supervision.

💡

Motivation

In Jordan, increased tablet usage among young children has reduced human interaction and negatively affected language development. Most Arabic apps focus on entertainment rather than therapy, while international solutions remain costly and culturally unadapted to the Arab world.

⚙️

System Features

  • Secure role-based authentication system
  • Interactive speech exercises for children
  • Session scheduling & appointment management
  • Real-time parent–specialist messaging
  • Progress tracking & advanced analytics
  • Achievements & gamification rewards
  • Comprehensive reports & dashboards
🎯

Project Objective

To provide a simple, secure, and effective platform for early speech rehabilitation with professional supervision, culturally adapted Arabic content, and full data privacy — making quality speech therapy accessible and affordable for all Jordanian families.
